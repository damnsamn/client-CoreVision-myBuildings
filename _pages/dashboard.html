Welcome back!

Spirit of history. Modern drive. ML7 is Australia's leading commercial hub for industry ground-breakers and trailblazers.

Built in 1972, ML7 was recently renovated to include an atrium, new community spaces and public-facing cafe. Architecturally designed with elegance in mind, Locus One marries sleek and stylish form with practical features and functionality including well-appointed end-of-trip facilities and gym.

The Locus One Concierge and Building Management team are here to help and have insider knowledge on the best places to eat, drink, stay and play nearby.

Whether you've worked in this neighbourhood for years or have recently made the move, we bet there's something you've never noticed before. The office is not just the few rooms you work in Monday to Friday — it spills out of the front doors of the building and stretches into the streets and businesses that surround us.

Go ahead and explore new areas. Get in touch with your community.
